**Course Checklist**

**The following materials are required for the course:**

**Promotional materials:**

Promo video (to market the course to tell them about the course and get them excited) 1-2 minutes. The promotional video should include the following:

Course title

What the course will be about, what they will learn and its application to the industry

A brief introduction of yourself

Mention that it is free of cost

Who can enroll

Sign up at: JournalismCourses.org

Mention of sponsors, if any.

Welcome video (introducing the course, who will be teaching and what) 2-3 minutes

Introductory reading (1-2)

Class syllabus (outlining what they will learn each week with clear goals/objectives)

Bio (of all instructors) of 200-300 words

Photo (of all instructors)

Welcome message for the students

**Overall course materials**

**For each week the following is needed:**

Welcome message of what they will learn (template attached)

2-3 forum questions (questions that reflect on the readings/videos to share their experiences with others, global in focus helps considering student body)

Quiz of 5 questions (multiple choice/T or F with the same number of responses for each question that reflect on learning outcomes for the week)
